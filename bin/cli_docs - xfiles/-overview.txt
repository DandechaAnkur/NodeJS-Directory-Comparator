
--------------
  -overview
--------------

  use:
  ---

    it is used to view the minimal details - only the total counts
    & the total sizes of - the mutually exclusive files.

  usage:
  -----

    xfiles  dir_path_1  dir_path_2  -overview
    xfiles  dir_path_1  dir_path_2  -o

    this command can also be used as the sub-command to the -filter
    or the -f command.
