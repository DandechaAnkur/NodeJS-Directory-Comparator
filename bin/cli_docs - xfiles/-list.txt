
----------
  -list
----------

  use:
  ---

    used for listing the paths of the mutually exclusive files of the
    2 directories.

  usage:
  -----

    xfiles  dir_path_1  dir_path_2  -list
    xfiles  dir_path_1  dir_path_2  -l

    this command can also be used as the sub-command to the -filter
    or the -f command.
